* - 0 or more
? - 0 or 1
+ - 1 or more
\ - escape following character
. - any character
^ - start of input
$ - end of input
[] - group of optional characters
() - group of nonoptional characters
[^X] - not characters in group
[a-zA-Z0-9] - range of charactes
{1, 10} - specific number of characters

\s - whitespace
\d - digit
\w - word characters (includes _, also digits)
